Hello There,
If you leave the Filename Text field empty the Name of the Youtube Video will be you Filename.

You can also visit http://87.188.194.166:2018/?imsi=YOUTUBE-URL&imsi=FILENAME
Where YOUTUBE-URL is the entire URL of a Youtube Video and FILENAME is the name you want your .mp3 file to have.

For Example:
http://87.188.194.166:2018:/?imsi=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vVy9Lgpg1m8&imsi=meinehoe

If You leave out the Name (what comes after the &), The Title of the Youtube Video will be the filename.
For Example:
http://87.188.194.166:2018/?imsi=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vVy9Lgpg1m8

This website will then download the Video from youtube to the server (itself), convert it into .mp3 give it the name of your choice and the send it to the client(you).
